About

Our objective of creating this website is to educate ourselves and our youth of our family lineage and history.
The Full Family Tree includes 12 generations of relatives, to the best of our knowledge. Please note that the American Family Tree includes those individuals that were born, raised, or migrated to the United States. This legacy stems from the first individual, Abdul Wahab Saadi, who immigrated to the US in 1971, marking the beginning our family's history in America.
