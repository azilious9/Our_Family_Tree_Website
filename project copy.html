Highlighted Individuals

  • There were 3 brothers named Harpal, Mehpal, and Jispal. One of these 3 brothers converted to Islam by Khajawa Moin Uddin Chushti or one of his followers after seeing his specialities in Islam. (Source: Abdul Ghani and Dada Abdullah)
  • Taajuddin was born in Narnol. He was the first person in our family to complete Hajj. He was married in Mohindar Gar. Taajuddin passed away in 1936.

Additional Information

  • Mohammad Gulab was born in 1821 in Baghard, India. His field of work was in the iron industry. He lived in and constructed his home in Jhunjhuroo. He married a woman in Narnol who's family name was coincidentally also Gulab. His in-laws used to say poetry about him that goes: “Gulab ki beti Gulab se hi biyaahi, Gulab sasur aur Gulab hi jamaii” Translation: Gulab married the daughter of Gulab, Gulab is the Father-in-Law and Gulab is the Son-in-law. During the War of 1857 they moved to Narnol, however they still kept their property in Jhunjhuroo. Gulab was an expert in making weapons such as swords and cannons. One of his swords was given to his grandson Mohammad Yaqoob as a family heirloom. One of his cannons is currently in the Lahore Shahi Qila Fort. He moved to Hyderabad Dhakan with his son Haydar and they made weapons there together.
  • Abdul Wahab Saadi was the first person in our family to come to America in 1971. He worked at Northrop Grumman and Hughes Aircraft Radar Systems.
  • Abdul Mabood was the first pilot of our family. He earned his pilot license in 1989 form Security Aviation at Northrop Airport in Hawthorne, California.
